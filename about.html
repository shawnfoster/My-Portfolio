About Me!

Even though I was seven years late to become a Navy SEAL, and ultimately could not continue to pursue due to an injury, I gained the support of a Senator and a Congressman that had agreed to back me in a congressional hearing. As with everything else that I have ever wanted to do, I’ve finally succeeded in meeting my goal of becoming a Full Stack Web Developer.
